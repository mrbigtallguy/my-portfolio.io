Woolworths Service Desk UI

The Problem

Communication issues combined with a frustrating and unintuitive customer experience when using the service desk at Woolworths.

The Method

Through a combination of user research methods, the team and I discovered three key areas needing improvement; clarity of purpose, as well as reliability and functionality of process.

The Solution

A self-service UI at the Woolworths service desk that processed simple aspects of a request to and allowed staff to focus on more complex, emotional aspects of the customer experience.

Nurse Connect

The Problem

VAD (Ventricular Assist Device) nurses experience an immense amount of emotional fatigue due to the demands of their job. Over time, this affects their overall health and their effectiveness at providing optimal paitient care.

The Method

The team and I were given access to detailed research which, upon analysis, provided key insights into the deeper issues concerning VAD nurse emotional faitgue.

The Solution

A digital interface that facilatates communication between VAD nurses and coordinators as well as the greater VAD community

Go-Hub

The Problem

Traffic Congestion during the work commute is frustrating and poorly affects someones day.

The Method

A combination of Report Research, Surveys and Contextual Interviews revealed the need for a digital mobile solution that provides optimal travel routes while automating the planning of these routes

The Solution

A transport planning application that integrates with an autonomous vehicle network to improve connections between public transport hubs and the users home.

The Team

  • Me - UX, Animation
  • Mikelis Knochs – UI, UX, Video Production
